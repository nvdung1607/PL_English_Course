Let's think! 🤔

Before we start, think about the following question and post your thoughts in the class discussion forum:

"What is the most important thing your parents do to help you with your studies?"

Suggested ideas:

  • They provide me with necessary tools like books and a laptop.
  • They prepare nutritious meals to keep me healthy.
  • They always listen to my problems and cheer me up when I'm stressed.

📚 New Vocabulary
